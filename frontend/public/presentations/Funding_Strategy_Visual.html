Dear Committee,

This document is our internal planning reference — aligned with the one-pager we are presenting to the batch. It covers our contribution projections, attendance assumptions, funding allocation, and the scenarios we need to plan around.

Our ₱2.1M target is achievable through the hybrid model — contributions as the foundation, fundraising initiatives as the multiplier. The projections here are honest. The conservative scenario is a reminder that the flyer alone won't get us there. Personal outreach from each of us is what moves people from awareness to commitment.

Thank you for being part of this.
