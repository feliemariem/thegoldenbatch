Bianca Jison
USLS-IS High School Batch 2003
Now Batch 2003 Representative to the USLS Alumni Association Officers and Board of Directors for SY 2026–2027
2028 President of the USLS Alumni Association Bacolod, Inc. during our 25th Jubilee Homecoming

The Organizing Committee proposes Bianca Jison as our Batch Representative in recognition of the initiative she has shown in proactively taking on many of the responsibilities associated with the role.

She has been coordinating with the organizing committee and the alumni office, helping organize our batch and move preparations forward for our 25th Jubilee.

While the Organizing Committee believes Bianca can effectively fulfill this role, we recognize and value every batchmate's voice in this decision. If you have someone else in mind who you feel is equally capable and willing to take this on, we encourage you to put their name forward. This is the batch's decision as much as it is ours.

📍 Please note: At the request of the USLS Alumni Association, the Batch Representative is preferred to be locally based in Bacolod, as this person will be required to attend board meetings in person and will eventually serve as Alumni Association President during our 25th Jubilee in 2028.
  • Represent Batch 2003 in the USLS Alumni Association Officers and Board of Directors for SY 2026–2027
  • Attend regular alumni board meetings in person at the Alumni Office, USLS, Bacolod City
  • Act on behalf of the batch in all Alumni Association matters for the stated term
  • Coordinate with the USLS Alumni Association on batch-related concerns and updates
  • Serve as the official liaison between Batch 2003 and the alumni office
  • Assume the position of President of the USLS Alumni Association Bacolod, Inc. during our 25th Jubilee in 2028
  • Preside over the General Alumni Homecoming as the hosting batch president in December 2028
  • Work closely with the organizing committee on all preparations leading up to the 25th Jubilee Homecoming
  • Represent the batch in school and community engagements as needed
  • Help drive participation, fundraising, and engagement among batchmates in the lead-up to 2028
💪 The Batch Representative will not be starting from scratch. There is already an organizing committee in place that has been working since 2023 — fully committed and ready to support whoever takes on this role.
